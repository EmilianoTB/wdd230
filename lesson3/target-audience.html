Target Audience


The audience for this local Chamber of Commerce website includes a diverse group of individuals and organizations with an interest in the local business community in Cuautla, Morelos. These include:

  • Local business owners and entrepreneurs looking for information and resources to help them grow and succeed
  • Potential investors and businesses looking to relocate to the area, seeking information on the local economy and business environment
  • Local government officials and community leaders seeking information on the economic health of the area and opportunities and challenges facing the community
  • Residents of Cuautla and the surrounding area who wants to keep informed about local events, community development projects, and other relevant news and information.
  • It is also designed to be accessible to anyone in the community, making it easy for anyone to access and navigate the website.

Overall, the website aims to be a valuable resource for anyone with an interest in the local business community and the economic development of Cuautla, Morelos.


Scenarios


  • A local business owner is looking for information on local regulations and permits required to operate their business in Cuautla, Morelos. The website can provide this information by including a section on local business resources, such as information on permits, licenses, and other regulations, as well as contact information for relevant government agencies.
  • A resident is looking for information on upcoming local events and community development projects in Cuautla, Morelos. The website can provide this information by including a calendar of events, as well as a section on community development projects, including updates on their progress and opportunities for community input and participation.
