News Release 📰

The Cuautla Chamber of Commerce is excited to announce the launch of our new Small Business Grant Program, designed to provide financial assistance to small businesses in our community. Through this program, eligible small businesses can receive grants of up to $5,000 to help cover expenses such as rent, utilities, and inventory. The grants are intended to provide a much-needed financial boost to small businesses that have been impacted by the COVID-19 pandemic.
