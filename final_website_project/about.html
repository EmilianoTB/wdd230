Bountiful Foods is a small business located in Carlsbad, California, specializing in providing fresh and healthy food options to the local population and tourists visiting the coastal areas. Our mission is to offer organic, locally grown foods, including made-to-order fruit drinks and fruit bags, directly from the farmers in the area. We also use some national and international suppliers to ensure we can provide our customers with a wide range of fresh and healthy food options.

More about us...

Our storefront in Carlsbad caters to walk-in traffic, where customers can find a wide variety of fruits and produce. We also have many food trucks that cover the coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego, making our fresh and healthy food options easily accessible to everyone.


At Bountiful Foods, we believe that healthy eating should be convenient, which is why we offer delivery and manage orders both on the phone and via our website. We pride ourselves on providing our customers with the freshest, healthiest food options possible, and we're committed to supporting local farmers and promoting organic bounty.

We're excited to announce that we're also located in Laguna Beach! Come visit us at either of our locations to experience our fresh and healthy food options firsthand.
